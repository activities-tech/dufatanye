About Our Mosque

DUFATANYE MARKAZ came to life in 2015 to serve the growing Rwandan Muslim community, and Muslims worldwide. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and community building.

We offer daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah services, Quran classes for all ages, and various community programs throughout the year.

Join us in our mission to foster a strong, united community based on the principles of Islam.

We are located at Rue van Lint 53, 1070 Anderlecht, Brussels.
